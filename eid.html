EID Philosophy & Commitment

I am committed to fostering an inclusive scientific community where diverse perspectives are valued and everyone has equal opportunities to contribute and succeed. Through my roles in scientific societies and outreach initiatives, I strive to:

  • Promote visibility of underrepresented groups in geosciences
  • Create accessible scientific content for global audiences
  • Support early-career researchers from diverse backgrounds
  • Facilitate international collaboration and knowledge exchange
  • Challenge biases and barriers in scientific communities
